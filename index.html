The Pig Game

  • The game has 2 players, playing in rounds.
  • In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as he wishes. Each result get added to his current score.
  • But, if the player rolls a 1, all his current score gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn.
  • The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his current score gets added to his overall score. After that, it's the next player's turn.
  • The first player to reach 100 points on overall score wins the game.
